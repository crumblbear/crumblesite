Services

Data and Goals Assessment

Data is stored in different places, shapes, and formats which makes it difficult to perform comprehensive analysis. CrumbleBear partners with clients to understand their goals and current datasets. A feasibility plan is created with recommendations and bite sized projects.

Data Extraction/Transformation/Loading

Most data projects begin with data extraction, transformation, and loading. This step provides clients with a model to better consume and act on their data. CrumbleBear provides clients with the flexibility to decide whether they want to run this process manually or work with us on an automated solution.

Process Automation/Reporting

At CrumbleBear we take advantage of system APIs, Microsoft Power Platform, programming, and other existing resources to automate processes. This service allows clients to spend more time serving their mission rather than pulling together data for reporting.

Business Intelligence/Visualization

Through visualization, organizations can establish a source of truth in an effortless, collaborative, actionable, timely, and consumable manner. This brings immediate value to decision makers and staff, ultimately sparking innovation and continuous improvement. When employees visualize their value to the organization, amazing things happen! CrumbleBear is here to establish and support that effort.

System Integration

Most clients implement systems to handle specific business tasks (CRM, Accounting, HR, Payroll, Social Media, etc.) but have no way of connecting the data stored in each product. To meet client goals, data from multiple sources need to be linked together for insight. A common scenario sees marketing running a social media campaign in hopes of bringing new donors. Social media accounts provide impression reporting but no donation activity. CrumbleBear links multiple data sources together to determine campaign effectiveness and improve future efforts.

About
